Welcome to my webpage!

I'm a machine learning engineer at [Fomoro AI](https://fomoro.com/), where I work on consulting projects and basic / applied research.

I received my M.Sc. in computer science from [UMass Amherst](http://www.umass.edu/) [CICS](http://www.cics.umass.edu/), where I also completed undergraduate degrees in computer science and mathematics. My studies and research focused on machine learning and computational neuroscience. I also studied computational complexity, statistics, causality, dynamical systems, and information theory.

During my masters studies, I was a research assistant in the [BINDS lab](http://binds.cs.umass.edu/), where we focused on developiing spiking neural networks (SNNs) for machine learning. I was involved in building and training SNNs to do image classification and reinforcement learning, transferring artificial neural networks to SNNs while maintaining good performance, and building a Python library for (GPU-enabled) SNN prototyping and simulation. 

Check out my [CV](cv/cv.pdf) for more details, and feel free to send me an [email](mailto:danjsaund@gmail.com) if you'd like to connect.
