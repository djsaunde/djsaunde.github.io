Welcome to my webpage!

I am currently a machine learning engineer at [Fomoro AI](https://fomoro.com/).

I received my M.Sc. in computer science at the [University of Massachusetts Amherst](http://www.umass.edu/) in the [CS department](http://www.cics.umass.edu/). I did my undergraduate studies in computer science and mathematics, also at UMass. The focus of my studies / research was on machine learning and theoretical / computational neuroscience, and how they are related. I also studied computational complexity, statistics, causality, dynamical systems, and information theory.

During my masters studies, I was a research assistant in the [BINDS lab](http://binds.cs.umass.edu/), where we focused on developing spiking neural networks (SNNs) for machine learning. I was involved in constructing and training SNNs to do image classification, transferring trained artificial neural networks to SNNs while maintaining performance, and building a Python library for (GPU-enabled) SNN prototyping and simulation. 

Please check out my [CV](cv/cv.pdf) for more details, and feel free to send me an [email](mailto:djsaunde@cs.umass.edu) if you'd like to talk.
